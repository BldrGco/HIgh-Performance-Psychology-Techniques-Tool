High-Performance Mindset

Simple tricks to level up your brain game!

Mental Contrasting

Dream big, then spot the roadblocks. Picture your goal—like acing a test—then think, “What might trip me up?” It’s like planning a superhero mission!

Identity-Based Reinforcement

Act like the person you want to be. Instead of “I’ll try to exercise,” say, “I’m a fit person who moves daily.” It’s your new superhero identity!

Stress Inoculation Training

Practice tough stuff in small doses. Like a video game boss—start with mini challenges (e.g., deep breaths in chaos) to build your calm muscle.

Decision Precommitment

Plan your moves before trouble hits. Decide now, “If I’m tempted to scroll, I’ll grab a book instead.” It’s your brain’s cheat code!

Success Autobiography

Write a story as your future awesome self. “In 2030, I’m a confident leader who crushed it!” It’s like scripting your own victory movie.
