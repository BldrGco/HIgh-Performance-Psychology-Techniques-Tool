High-Performance Mindset

Smash your limits with these brain hacks!

Mental Contrasting

Dream big, then spot the roadblocks. Picture your goal—like acing a test—then think, “What might trip me up?” Plan your victory!

Identity-Based Reinforcement

Act like your best self. Don’t just try—say, “I’m a powerhouse who gets it done!” Own it!

Stress Inoculation Training

Face small stressors head-on—like deep breaths in chaos—to build unbreakable calm.

Decision Precommitment

Pre-plan your moves. “If I waver, I’ll strike back with focus!” Rig the game in your favor.

Success Autobiography

Script your future win. “In 2030, I’m a titan who conquered all!” Make it real.
